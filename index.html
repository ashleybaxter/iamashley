Hey, I'm Ashley. I'm an unusual hybrid of photographer and insurer, with a smattering of programming on the side. I run Insurance by Jack, tailoring business insurance to creative professionals. Come the summer months, I can be found photographing a limited number of weddings. I also record a weekly podcast where—alongside my co-host—we discuss our journeys in creating our passion-based businesses. I like video games, the gym, and my dog. I tweet at @iamashley.
